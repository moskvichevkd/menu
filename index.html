Our History
Like any other distinctive place, we have our own, special history. The idea of the restaurant came to the founders unexpectedly. While walking through the forest, the creator of our restaurant got stuck hundreds of kilometers from the nearest settlement. Far from civilization and communication, they had to arrange a simple life for themselves for a while, get and cook their own food.
